Our Story

Fourteen years of baking love into every crumb — Jalandhar's most cherished bakery.

Chef Manpreet Kaur – Founder
Our Founder

Baked With Passion, Served With Pride

Velvet Crumb Café was founded in 2010 by Chef Manpreet Kaur, a Le Cordon Bleu trained pastry chef with roots in Jalandhar and a dream to bring world-class baking to her city.

What began as a small home bakery on Model Town soon grew into Jalandhar's most loved destination for premium cakes, custom cakes, and eggless specialities. Chef Manpreet's philosophy is simple: never compromise on ingredients, never rush a bake, and never stop learning.

Today, Velvet Crumb Café serves over 3,500 customers monthly from its flagship store in Jalandhar, with a team of 18 passionate bakers and cake artists. We are proud to be consistently voted the best cafe in Jalandhar across Google, Zomato, and local food surveys.

Our Journey

Milestones That Made Us

From a home kitchen to Jalandhar's most awarded bakery — here's how our story unfolded.

2010
Velvet Crumb Café is Born

Chef Manpreet begins baking custom cakes from her home kitchen in Model Town, Jalandhar. Word spreads fast.

2013
First Physical Store

We open our first storefront on Model Town, Jalandhar. Demand for eggless cakes drives 40% of our early business.

2016
Wedding Cake Division

Launch of our dedicated bridal cake service, becoming the go-to bakery for wedding cakes in Jalandhar.

2019
Best Bakery Award

Voted "Best Cafe in Jalandhar" at the Punjab Food & Hospitality Awards. Featured in Punjab Kesari and Tribune.

2023
Online Ordering Launch

Launched online ordering and same-day delivery across all of Jalandhar. Now serving 3,500+ orders monthly.
