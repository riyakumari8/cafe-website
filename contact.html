FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer same-day cake delivery in Jalandhar?

Yes! We offer same-day delivery for standard cakes ordered before 2:00 PM. For custom cakes, we require at least 3–5 days notice. We deliver across all areas of Jalandhar.

Are all your cakes available as eggless?

Yes, every cake on our menu — including all custom cakes — is available as a 100% eggless cake at no extra cost. Just tick the eggless option on your order form or mention it when you call.

How far in advance should I order a custom cake?

For standard cakes: 1–2 days. For custom single-tier cakes: 3–5 days. For wedding cakes or multi-tier cakes: 2–3 weeks. Read our full custom cake guide for more tips.

What is the minimum order for a custom cake?

Our minimum custom cake size is 0.5 kg for a single-tier design. Prices start at ₹1,299/kg. Multi-tier wedding cakes have a minimum of 3 kg. See our custom cakes pricing page for full details.
